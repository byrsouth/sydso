About

Robyn Rihanna Fenty, born February 20, 1988, in St. Michael, Barbados, is an international pop icon.

Rihanna also has a heavy influence in the makeup and fashion industries after launching her makeup and clothing lines in 2017.

With nine Grammy Awards under her belt, Rihanna's "Navy" fans are waiting for her new music release with great anticipation.

Rihanna's "Anti World Tour" will have 75 shows in 70 different countries, and is expected to be the mogul's greatest tour yet.
